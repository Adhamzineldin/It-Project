About Invertebrates

From the depths of the ocean to your own backyard, invertebrates rule the world! They make up over 95% of all animal species on Earth, showcasing an incredible array of shapes, sizes, and behaviors.

Invertebrates play vital roles in ecosystems around the globe. As pollinators, decomposers, predators, and prey, they are the unsung heroes of our planet.
