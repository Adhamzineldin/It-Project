Garden Snail

The garden snail, also known as Cornu aspersum, is one of the most common snail species found in gardens around the world. It has a characteristic spiral shell and is often considered a pest by gardeners.

Apple Snail

Apple snails, belonging to the genus Pomacea, are freshwater snails known for their large size and colorful shells. They are popular in freshwater aquariums but can also be invasive in some ecosystems.

Roman Snail

The Roman snail, or Helix pomatia, is a land snail native to Europe. It is prized as a delicacy in some cuisines and is also known for its role in producing cochineal dye.
