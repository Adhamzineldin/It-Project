About Crocodiles

Crocodiles are large aquatic reptiles that live throughout the tropics in Africa, Asia, the Americas, and Australia.

Crocodiles are primarily nocturnal predators, spending most of the day resting or basking in the sun.

Crocodiles inhabit freshwater habitats such as rivers, lakes, and wetlands, as well as brackish water habitats.

Crocodiles are carnivorous and eat a variety of prey including fish, birds, mammals, and other reptiles.

To learn more about crocodiles, visit Wikipedia's Crocodile Page .
