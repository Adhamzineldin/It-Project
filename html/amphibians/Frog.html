All About Frogs

Frogs are amphibians known for their jumping abilities and their unique life cycle.

They come in various shapes, sizes, and colors, with some species even capable of changing their skin color.

Frogs play an essential role in the ecosystem as both predator and prey. They typically start their lives as tadpoles, living underwater and breathing through gills, before metamorphosing into adult frogs with lungs.

Frogs are found in almost every ecosystem worldwide, from tropical rainforests to deserts.
